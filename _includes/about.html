Shane's open source history

  • November 1999

    Apache committer at the start

    Committer and PMC member on the Apache Xalan project, one of the first few projects created after the incorporation of the ASF in June 1999.

  • 2002-2008

    ApacheCon organizing committee

    After volunteering just once for the "Concom" committee, I organized ApacheCon conferences, and served as conference lead for ApacheCon US 2008 in New Orleans.

  • 2009-2015

    Director / VP, Brand

    After being elected to the Board of Directors in 2009, I led the reorganization of the ASF's public relations committee into individual, delegated roles with clear responsibilities.

  • 2016

    The new beginning

    With extensive experience with technology, communities, branding, and conference speaking, it's time to start the next step: Punderthings Consulting: new ways to engage in open source!
